## Common bottlenecks

High-write paths often suffer from hidden lock amplification and poor index selectivity.

## Practical strategies

- Keep write transactions short.
- Push heavy side effects off critical write path.
- Use query plans as first-class observability artifacts.

## Measuring success

Track lock wait time, p95 latency, and throughput as a combined health signal.
