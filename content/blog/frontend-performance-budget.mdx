## Set explicit budgets

Budgeting prevents performance regressions from becoming invisible until late.

## Budget categories

- JavaScript payload size.
- Largest contentful paint.
- Critical image weight.

## Team workflow

Treat performance budgets as a release gate, not an aspirational guideline.
