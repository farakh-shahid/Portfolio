## Why modular monolith first

A modular monolith allows strong boundaries without distributed system overhead too early.

## Boundary rules that matter

- Explicit module ownership.
- No cross-module data leakage.
- Events for side effects rather than direct coupling.

## Migration path

Extract modules only when operational constraints demand distribution.
