## Problem

Enterprise customers required strict isolation and auditability that a single-tenant design could not provide.

## Approach

- Propagated tenant context through request boundaries.
- Added policy checks at service and query layers.
- Built onboarding automation with consistent baseline controls.

## Outcome

Enabled secure enterprise growth without major architecture rewrites.
