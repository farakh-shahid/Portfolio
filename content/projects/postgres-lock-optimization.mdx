## Problem

Write traffic spikes introduced cascading lock waits due to synchronous trigger execution on shared rows.

## Approach

- Profiled blocking query chains and lock wait trees.
- Moved non-critical side effects to worker-driven asynchronous processing.
- Introduced targeted compound indexes for high-cardinality access paths.

## Outcome

The system regained predictable latency and sustained peak load windows without user-visible degradation.
